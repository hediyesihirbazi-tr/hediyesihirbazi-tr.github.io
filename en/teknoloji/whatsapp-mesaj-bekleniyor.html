How to Fix WhatsApp "Waiting for this message" Error (2026)

Feb 09, 2026 ‚Ä¢ Tech ‚Ä¢ 3 min read

You changed your phone or reinstalled WhatsApp. You open a chat, but instead of the text, you see: "Waiting for this message. This may take a while."

This is due to end-to-end encryption. The unique security key between your phone and the sender's phone hasn't synced yet.

üõ†Ô∏è Solution 1: Trigger Backup (Fastest Way)

This trick forces WhatsApp servers to refresh the security keys.

  1. Go to WhatsApp Settings > Chats > Chat Backup.
  2. Tap "Back Up Now".
  3. Let it start even for 1%. This triggers a server sync.
  4. Go back to the chat. The message should appear instantly.

Solution 2: Ask Sender to Open WhatsApp

The most common reason is that the sender's phone is offline. The encrypted message is locked and needs their phone to be "Online" to send the key.

  • Call or SMS your friend: "Can you open WhatsApp for a second?"
  • As soon as they open the app, the keys match and the text unlocks.

Solution 3: WhatsApp Web Sync

Sometimes the mobile app gets stuck, but the Web version forces a sync.

  • Go to web.whatsapp.com on your PC.
  • Scan the QR code with your phone.
  • While the web version fetches history, it often pushes the stuck messages to your phone too.
